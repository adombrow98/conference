Study of Botany Conference

Alison is a plant enthusiast and animal lover. She learned a lot of what she knows from her college professor who got her interested in botany and how plants can benefit people. She enjoys gardening and house plants as well. She finds it interesting the various ways plants can be used to cure ailments, cause problems, or improve mental health. The one biggest thing that she did not realize at first is how important fungi are and that they have so many different possible uses. A lot of her interest lies in mental health improvement and cures for diseases such as cancer.
