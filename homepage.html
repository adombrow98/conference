Study of Botany Conference

New scientific discoveries are being made daily. Making it easier for one to live longer and be healthier. Oddly enough, a lot of discoveries often come from nature itself and learning how to use it. That is why this conference is focused on the botanist side of science and learning about how everyday plants could benefit everyone in the long run. By studying this we could learn various ways to treat illness that does not rely on testy chemicals or otherwise harmful materials. This years conference takes place on the 29th July at the Greater Richmond Convention Center.
